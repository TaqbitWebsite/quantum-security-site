Quantum Key Distribution (QKD)

Our QKD systems enable two parties to produce a shared random secret key known only to them, providing information-theoretic security.

  • Proven security against quantum attacks
  • Detects any eavesdropping attempts
  • Operates over standard fiber optic networks

Quantum Random Number Generator (QRNG)

Generates true entropy from photon-level quantum randomness — ideal for secure cryptographic systems and applications requiring perfect unpredictability.

  • True quantum randomness (not pseudorandom)
  • Certified for cryptographic applications
  • High-speed generation (up to 10 Gbps)
