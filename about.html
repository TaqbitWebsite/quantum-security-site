Our Story

Taqbit Labs was founded in 2018 by a team of quantum physicists from IIT Madras who recognized the urgent need for quantum-resistant security solutions.

What began as academic research into quantum key distribution quickly evolved into a mission to protect critical infrastructure from the coming quantum computing threat.

Today, we're proud to be India's leading quantum cybersecurity company, with deployments across defense, government, and enterprise sectors.
